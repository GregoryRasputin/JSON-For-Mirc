Feed Back

If you have a suggestion or believe you've encountered a bug feel free to open an issue ticket on the project's github. Suggestions are always welcome and bugs are a top priority; as such the issue tracker is checked daily and all open tickets are addressed immediately.

Without user feedback there can be no developer progression

Chat

If you need help, are wondering about updates, or just want to see what this project is all about, feel free to join any of the offical chat platforms available:

  • IRC: SwiftIRC.net's #JSONForMirc channel (webclient) [Recommended]
  • IRC: Freenode's #JSONForMirc channel (webclient)
  • Discord
  • Gitter

Though the topic of these channels is JSON For mIRC, open discussion about most topics is allowed and encouraged
